# Privacy Policy for Dueling Batteries

**Effective Date:** 12/17/2025

Dueling Batteries is a Wear OS watch face built using Watch Face Format.
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

## Information Collection and Use

- This watch face does **not collect, store, or share any personal information**.
- It does not access contacts, location, health data, or any other user-identifiable information.
- The app only displays time and design elements as intended by the watch face format.

## Third-Party Services

- This watch face does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.
- This watch face may display data provided by third‑party complication apps (e.g., phone battery level). However, it does not collect or transmit any information to those apps.

## Children’s Privacy

- Since no data is collected, the watch face is safe for all users, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

- If we ever update the watch face to include features that require data access, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.
